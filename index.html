The Delivery Rule

Multiply the outside number by every single term inside the brackets — nobody gets skipped. Watch the signs closely; they travel with the delivery.

a(b + c) = ab + ac

The courier a knocks on both doors: it multiplies b, then it multiplies c. Two separate deliveries, one trip.

If a is negative, both deliveries flip sign — that's the part most people forget to check.

+×+=+

2(3x + 1)
2 × 3x = 6x
2 × 1 = 2
Answer: 6x + 2
